Spectral Insight was founded by people who knew Niagara vineyards before they knew hyperspectral. We work for growers, not platforms.

01 · STORY

It started in a Cab Franc block.

In 2022, one of our founders was walking a vineyard in the Beamsville Bench when the owner pointed at a perfectly healthy-looking row and said: "something's wrong there, but I can't tell you why."

Two months later, half the row came down with leafroll. The vines had been signalling stress for weeks — in wavelengths the human eye simply cannot see.

Spectral Insight exists because that conversation has happened in every wine region, every year, for the last century. And because — finally — we have the satellites to end it.

02 · WHAT WE BELIEVE

Four rules we run on.

I

Answers, not data.

A grower does not want a GeoTIFF. A grower wants to know where to walk this afternoon. We exist to close that gap.

II

Pick up the phone.

Every client has a direct line to a human who knows their property. No ticket queues, no bot triage, no "we'll get back to you."

III

Local first.

We do one thing — Ontario vineyards — and we do it carefully. Generic tools trained on Iowa corn are why growers stopped trusting remote-sensing fifteen years ago.

IV

Honest about limits.

Clouds exist. Snow exists. Some weeks we won't have a clean pass. We'd rather tell you so than fabricate certainty.
